    Open source is for feedback

    3 minute read

    Open source is at the core of AEM.

    Supporting the community is the core principle of AEM.Design. AEM Design has always been about providing principled design patterns for AEM, that work in practice. Our components are tested through practice and implemented so that you can use them the way you want. We aim at developing a component that is free of noise and delivers exactly what you are looking for which is a clean, lean HTML footprint that speaks content and does not get on the way with opinions.

    We strongly believe that open source provides the most transparent way of giving feedback about our work. We believe that having a common goal for creating and spreading good practices on making your AEM experience awesome. We do open source to encourage sharing of great things that can be done in AEM.
